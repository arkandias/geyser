As part of managing teaching service assignments for mathematics faculty at the Integral University, the Mathematics Department collects and processes your personal data based on your consent.

This information is securely stored on department servers for 5 years, without transmission to external services.

Data privacy:

  • Visible to all teachers: your teaching preferences (primary and secondary) and assignments.
  • Visible only to the assignment commission: your service modifications (teaching load reductions, etc.) and your message to the commission.

You have the right to access, rectify, delete, or limit the processing of your personal data. You may also withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. Visit cnil.fr for more information about your rights.

To exercise your rights or for any questions about the processing of your data, you may contact the Head of Mathematics Department:
